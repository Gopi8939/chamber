About IUECC

Building Bridges Between India and UAE for Global Growth

At IUECC, we are more than just a business association. Established on July 16, 2022, our Chamber is built on the foundation of facilitating cross-border collaboration between Indian and UAE businesses.

Our Mission

To create a robust platform that brings entrepreneurs, startups, and enterprises together, offering them the resources, networking opportunities, and guidance to expand their operations in both regions.

Our Vision

To be the leading enabler of economic growth, innovation, and collaboration between India and the UAE, fostering mutual prosperity and development.

Our Strategic Pillars

Your Gateway to Indo-UAE Business Collaboration

We are dedicated to fostering trade, innovation, and collaboration between the two countries, empowering entrepreneurs and businesses to thrive in a global marketplace.

  • Trade & Investment Promotion
    We work to create a seamless flow of trade and investment between India and the UAE, identifying new opportunities for our members.
  • Policy Advocacy
    We represent our members' interests to policymakers and advocate for policies that foster a business-friendly environment.
